I Am Sam

For the past 14 years, I have been working as a memorial mason, where I have honed my attention to detail and craftsmanship through to creating meaningful tributes for those who have lost those near to them. However, my passion for technology and web development has led me to embark on new journey into the world of tech.
I am deeply fascinated by technology and its potential to shape the future. It's this passion that motivated me to pursue web development. I am currently participating in a 16-week web development bootcamp to refine my skills and expand my knowledge.
